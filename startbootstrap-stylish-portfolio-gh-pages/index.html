Welcome to the Fall 2016 Hackathon hosted by ACM. For 12 hours, tens of tens of programmers from the local community will gather to collaboratively code in an extreme manner.

Each developer, whether a veteran or begineer, has the ability and freedom to work on whatever he/she desires. Supported by peers and mentors, ideas will enter our physical world by the labor of a hand and the creation of a mind. Fueled by an atmosphere of achievement, several brillant projects will be created. Your reward? Knowledge, power, self-impovement, untold glory.
